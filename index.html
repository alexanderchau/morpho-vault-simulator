How the math works
Idle % = max(0, 100% − Σ allocations)
Gross APY = Σ (allocation % × market APY)  — idle counts as 0%, dragging APY down
Net APY = Gross APY × (1 − performance fee)
Annual yield = Net APY × total vault size
